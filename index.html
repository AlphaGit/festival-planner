How this works
  1. Tag each film below, then press Create schedule.
  2. Answer the questions in the wizard — each choice narrows the schedule.
  3. You end with a conflict-free schedule to follow.
  • Must — takes absolute priority; kept at the cost of other films.
  • Want — desirable; kept when it fits.
  • Skip — discarded (the default).
